Colours

A cohesive colour palette is a crucial aspect of design that contributes to the overall success and impact of a project or brand. It enhances visual appeal, communicates messages effectively, and establishes a strong and consistent identity.

Green was selected because it is a versatile and widely used colour in design due to its strong connections with nature, its soothing qualities, and its ability to create a harmonious and balanced visual experience. Understanding the colour theory behind green allows designers to use it effectively in their projects to achieve specific emotional and psychological effects.

Buttons

Buttons are vital components of web design that facilitate user interaction, encourage specific actions, and contribute to the overall usability and success of a website. Their presence and effective design play a significant role in achieving the website's goals and providing a positive user experience.

I opted for Flat design over Skeuomorphic design because of its minimalistic approach to visual elements. Clean lines, simple shapes, and a minimalistic approach to design components embraces a modern aesthetic that aligns well with contemporary design trends and user preferences.

Fonts

I've chosen Work Sans and Playfair Display from Google Fonts as the primary font vessels for this project.
