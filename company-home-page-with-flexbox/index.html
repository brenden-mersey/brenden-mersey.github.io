Ribalta

Pizzeria Tradizionale

Nestled in Naples, Ribalta is an unassuming gem. Its rustic charm, vintage decor, and wood-fired oven offer an authentic Italian experience. The pizzas are works of art, meticulously crafted by expert pizzaiolos. Ribalta's Margherita and Vesuvio pizzas stand out, with a perfect blend of San Marzano tomatoes, creamy mozzarella, and a fiery kick. The warm staff treats guests like family, making personalized recommendations. Live Neapolitan music adds to the ambiance, transporting diners to the heart of Italy. Ribalta's dessert menu, featuring tiramisu and panna cotta, provides a sweet ending to a memorable meal. In Ribalta, tradition, flavor, and community come together to celebrate Italy's culinary heritage.
